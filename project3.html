DigIT Lab

Kyle Cornelius

Overview

DigIT is OptumRx's in house innovation department. The 3 core groups, Engagement, Engineering, and Experience Center share their innovations, services, and insight to help push Optum further into the future.

Challenge

DigIT lacked an effective and centralized way to publicize their engagement services, innovative prototypes, and experience center. As the priority of DigIT is outreach, this was hurting the team's ability to share their innovations.

Goal

Provide a centralized website where employees can learn what DigIT is, what it offers, and how they can get involved with services.

The website needed to have a home page that explained all 3 parts of DigIT as well as separate areas to dive deeper into the core function of each.

DigIT sucessfully created over 20 functional prototypes, interacted with near 4,000 employees, and has held dozens of events

Flow


During the design phase, the goal was to have a more interactive experience with minimal confusion. The 3 main parts of the site all have a similar click and float feel.
