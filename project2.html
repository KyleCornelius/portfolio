RxReferral

Kyle Cornelius

Overview

Optum Infusion Services has an at home treatment service to help those who need out-patient infusion therapy care. Optum is always looking for ways to better serve patients and improve the care experience.

Challenge

Many patients that receive infusion services are elderly. Our in-home nurses notice that most of their patients take multiple medications and although they may struggle to get around, still go to a retail pharmacy.

Goal

Help our patients by streamlining their medication, reduce their overall medication costs, and growing one of Optum's core business, Optum Home Delivery.

Instead of having to drive to a pharmacy, our infusion nurses can refer your medications so that your they arrive at our patients door step.

Optum Infusion services works with near 2,000 nurses, bringing infusion therapy to many across the country

Flow


During the design phase, I architected this document to show the flow of data with user interactions. This helped to identify what information was needed where and when in the user experience while also identifying that a singular flow was the best screen approach for this step by step process.

Roadblocks

During development business requirements changed making our initial flow and some designs have to change with it. As we could no longer process credit card info through our application we pivoted to an automated email with a scheduled call back to the patient.
